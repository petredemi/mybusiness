Why to have a website

Having a website is crucial for businesses and individuals alike as it offers numerous benefits, including increased credibility, enhanced visibility, and improved customer engagement. It provides a central hub for information, allowing for 24/7 access to your products, services, and brand story.

What can display a website

A website can display a wide variety of content and functionality, including text, images, videos, interactive elements, and more. It can be used to share information, promote a business, provide services, or create a community. Essentially, anything that can be represented digitally can be displayed on a website.

How to make a website

There are two main methods of building a website: using a website builder that offers pre-built templates, to which you add text and images, or through Coding which offers total freedom of design and functionality, but requires advanced knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, which we offer.

The costs of a website

The cost of a website varies greatly depending on several factors, the construction of the website depends on the complexity, such as the number of pages, whether it is for general presentation or online commerce to which it is added, the domain (website address), the storage of the site on a server and some security certificates depending on the complexity.

How to promote a site

To effectively promote a website, have focus on a multi-faceted approach including Search Engine Optimization (SEO), content marketing. Optimizing for search engines, creating valuable content, and actively engaging on social media platforms are key strategies for driving traffic and increasing visibility.
